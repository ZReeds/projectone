New Found Glory Performance:

I was in the front of the crowd at the New Found Glory concert I attended at The Wiltern. Chad Gilbert, the guitarist of the band, remembered me from past meet and greets and I heard him say “come up here to sing a song with us” and security helped me get on stage and I was given a microphone because I was about to sing a song with them. I knew the song that I was going to sing with them was Failure’s Not Flattering because throughout the entire tour, they had people from the crowd get up on stage and sing that song with them. And I’m singing and jumping around on stage throughout most of the song, until I was safely escorted by security back into the crowd.

  • Performed on: June 16, 2018
