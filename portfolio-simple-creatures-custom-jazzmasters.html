Simple Creatures’ Custom Jazzmasters

This is a sharpie sketch of Simple Creatures’ Custom Jazzmasters. Simple Creatures are a trash pop/synth pop duo consisting of Mark Hoppus from blink-182 and Alex Gaskarth from All Time Low, who formed in early 2019. Mark plays the white Jazzmaster on the left, while Alex plays the black Jazzmaster on the right during certain songs.
