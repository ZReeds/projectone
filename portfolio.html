Jack Fowler’s Gibson and Epiphone Les Paul Guitars:

This is a sharpie sketch of Sleeping With Sirens guitarist Jack Fowler’s Gibson and Epiphone Les Paul guitars. Jack Fowler has played these guitars all summer long with Sleeping With Sirens at Rockstar Energy Drink DISRUPT Festival. This piece of music art captures the images of each Les Paul guitar along with their details.
